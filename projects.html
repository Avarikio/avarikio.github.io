Honeybadger Ultralight

A hybrid-electric, single seat ultralight aircraft project, designed to Canadian Advanced Ultra-Light Aeroplane regulations. The completed aircraft will feature monocoque construction, a hybrid gasoline/electric drive located amidships, cruising at 120 knots with a range of approximately 500 nautical miles. An all-electric endurance of 30 minutes at maximum power is expected, enabling either electric assistance at takeoff or quiet electric flight in the terminal phases of flight for operation in noise-restricted areas. See the project page for more detailed information.

Third Thumb Prosthesis

Bracelet with integrated sensors, actuators, and structure supporting addition of a "third thumb" for dexterity improvements.

Adaptive Planform Development Code

Interfacing with Athena Vortex Lattice (AVL), uses a simplified genetic algorithm to develop wing and tail planforms for the conceptual development of aircraft, to specified performance and stability coefficients.
